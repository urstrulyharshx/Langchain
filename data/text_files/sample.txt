Company Internal Policy Document

This document describes the internal policies of Acme Tech Solutions.
It is intended for employees, contractors, and interns.

1. Working Hours
The standard working hours are from 9:30 AM to 6:30 PM, Monday to Friday.
Employees are expected to be available during core hours between 11:00 AM
and 4:00 PM. Flexible working hours may be approved by the manager.

2. Work From Home Policy
Employees are allowed to work from home up to two days per week.
Any additional work from home days require prior manager approval.
Remote work is subject to performance and project requirements.

3. Leave Policy
Employees are entitled to 20 paid leaves per year.
Unused leaves can be carried forward up to a maximum of 10 days.
Sick leave does not require prior approval but must be informed
to the reporting manager on the same day.

4. Code of Conduct
All employees must maintain professional behavior at the workplace.
Harassment, discrimination, or unethical behavior will not be tolerated
and may result in disciplinary action, including termination.

5. Data Security
Confidential company data must not be shared with external parties.
Employees must use company-approved devices and secure networks
when accessing internal systems.

6. Exit Policy
Employees must serve a notice period of 30 days.
Company assets must be returned on or before the last working day.
Final settlement will be processed within 45 days after exit.

End of Document
